The Deepfake Threat

Deepfakes utilize malicious artificial intelligence to map human faces and synthesize fake video/audio. From morphed celebrity videos to high-level corporate fraud, manipulated media is the weapon of choice for modern cybercriminals.

Cybercrime Alert: 400% increase in AI-generated identity theft in the last year.

Why Detection Matters

  • Protecting Identity: Preventing unauthorized facial recreation.
  • Combating Misinformation: Stopping the spread of fake news.
  • Securing Finance: Blocking KYC fraud and social engineering.
  • Restoring Trust: Verifying digital evidence in real-time.
